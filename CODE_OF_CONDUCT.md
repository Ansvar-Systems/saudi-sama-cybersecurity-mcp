# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and productive community for
everyone contributing to this MCP server.

## Our Standards

Behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Constructive feedback, given and received gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community and the code
- Empathy toward other community members

Behaviour that is not tolerated:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission

## Enforcement

Reports of unacceptable behaviour may be sent to **hello@ansvar.ai**. All
reports will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers will respond in a way
they deem appropriate to the circumstances.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith
may face temporary or permanent repercussions at the discretion of project
leadership.
